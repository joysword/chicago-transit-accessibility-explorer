The Metropolitan Chicago Accessibility Explorer is a project that was funded by IDOT's METSI program and the National Center for Transit Research NCTR-USF through UIC's Urban Transportation Center. The project was undertaken by the Travel Behavior and Urban Systems Research Group at the Department of Urban Planning and Policy at UIC.
