Welcome to the Metropolitan Chicago Accessibility Explorer!

This site gives you the ability to look at the number of jobs, groceries and other activities, which can be reached from different neighborhoods in the metro area within certain travel time thresholds by different modes.

Use the menu at the top to choose the activities you are interested in, the travel mode, and the time threshold by that mode. The map will show accessibilities at the blockgroup level. You can turn on the neighborhood/cities layer to make it easier to identify the areas you are interested in.

Once the map loads, hover the mouse the area that interests you to see the accessibility levels for that area.
